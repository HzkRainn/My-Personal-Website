What's Come Its Something To Uncover In The Future Ahead

"Live The Day by Bearing The Feel On The Past And Keep Moving Forward"

Where I am now is proof of my struggles all this time, with various decisions made in both normal and challenging situations, and life's journey that always reveals its unpredictable nature. This, of course, is also due to His blessings and guidance that have always been with me in every step, leading me to this point to see how my path unfolds towards my future, a journey I must tread carefully to look back upon with pride when the time comes.

Encountering various new situations and conditions and trying to navigate through them is not something that can be easily done. Meeting new challenges and comrades-in-arms signifies a new chapter in my journey, one that I'm just beginning with new companions. Though it's difficult, we must endure and keep growing to move forward and reach our individual dreams.

As time passes, we continue our journey through our college days together, day by day. After some time, adapting to our new environment holds memories of joy and excitement, along with the pains from our struggles. Yet, we strive to do our best, knowing that we still have time and a long journey ahead of us.

Often, I ponder if only in the past I could have tried harder, seen things from different perspectives, and reduced viewing things solely from my own point of view. I wish I could have put more effort into self-improvement rather than dwelling on regrets and pondering how everything could have been fixed. But sometimes not everything can be fixed or go as smoothly as we desire. There are many things I could have learned and how to make better decisions in any situation.

Nothing is perfect because everyone has their strengths and weaknesses. While it might seem easy to say what you've thought, it's truly challenging to implement those thoughts and face realities, as everything is dynamic and constantly changing, be it in conditions or time periods. However, what's happening now is a result of our past experiences, shaping where we are today. What comes next and the future we aim for are what we will navigate towards.

"I have great hope that in the future I can make all my dreams come true, fight for what I need to fight for, and continue improving myself gradually. I strive to do my best because I aim to be better than I was before, for the future that awaits me."
