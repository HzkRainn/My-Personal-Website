The Long Journey Passed by Until This Moment

"The Past That Made Who We Are Now"

The long journey filled with twists and unexpected events continues. Yet, this is why we keep living and moving forward, facing whatever life throws at us. What lies in the future for us to experience next is why we exist in the present, constantly stepping forward and anticipating what awaits us on the next leg of the journey.

From a childhood full of joy as well as pain and sorrow, I find myself here today. It's not just about the situations and conditions around me, but also the people in my life who continuously support me in this journey of life. Whether it's friends or family, they've always been there for me through thick and thin, up until now.

Currently, I am pursuing my studies at the Manado State Polytechnic, majoring in Electrical Engineering with a focus on Informatics. Of course, it's not as easy as simply entering a new environment and adapting to its dynamics. Meeting new people and adjusting to a new setting while navigating daily life is a challenge I face now.

Is it difficult? Certainly, it's not an easy task. It's not just about adapting but also about embracing the changes happening around me, changes that I wouldn't be aware of if I didn't experience them firsthand. However, whatever is happening now is part of my journey to reach this point. That's why I must go through it, be patient, and try to enjoy the learning process from what has been overcome and what lies ahead.

It may seem easy when described in words, but it's uncertain whether I can execute what I envision. Yet, just like before, what exists now must be faced and overcome to see the future that awaits me someday.

"What we have now is a result of decisions and experiences from the past. Everyone has their own perspective, path, and way of life. Sometimes, what we can't understand about our own journey can be understood by observing the experiences of others. Not everything needs to be experienced alone; sometimes, we need to be open to what's around us. However, we are the ones steering our life's journey; we decide which path to take and what decisions to make as we navigate through life."
